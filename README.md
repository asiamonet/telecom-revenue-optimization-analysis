# Telecom Revenue Optimization: Surf vs. Ultimate Plans

## Project Overview
This project analyzes consumer behavior for Megaline, a leading telecom operator. The goal is to determine which prepaid plan—**Surf** or **Ultimate**—generates higher revenue to help the commercial department optimize their 2026 advertising spend.

## Business Challenge
* **The Problem:** The commercial department needs to know where to allocate their marketing budget.
* **The Data:** A selection of 500 Megaline clients, including call logs, SMS records, and data usage from 2018.
* **The Goal:** Perform a preliminary analysis to identify the more profitable plan and explain why.

## Key Technical Skills Demonstrated
* **Data Aggregation:** Consolidating call, text, and data logs into a monthly revenue table.
* **Statistical Analysis:** Applying hypothesis testing to determine if revenue differences are statistically significant.
* **Behavior Profiling:** Analyzing how "Surf" users differ from "Ultimate" users in their consumption habits.

## Tools Used
* **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)
* **Matplotlib** (Data Visualization)
* **SciPy** (Statistical Hypothesis Testing)
